# Product Principles

These principles serve as our north star. Whenever we're unsure about a feature, we check it against these principles.

1. **Human-first AI**
2. **Accessibility by default**
3. **Mobile-first**
4. **Premium UI**
5. **Fast by default**
6. **AI augments, not replaces**
7. **Every interaction teaches confidence**
8. **Every screen has one primary action**
9. **Less clutter, more clarity**
10. **Build for delight**
